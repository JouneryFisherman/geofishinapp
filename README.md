# 🌎 What Is Geo Fishin™?

Geo Fishin™ is more than an app — it’s a movement.  
A real‑world, GPS‑powered treasure hunt that blends outdoor exploration, fishing culture, and purpose-driven adventure.

Created by **Jeremy Reed — Journey Fisherman**, Geo Fishin™ invites people to step outside, discover hidden catches, earn rewards, and reconnect with the world around them.

---

# 🎣 What Makes Geo Fishin™ Different

Geo Fishin™ combines:

- Real‑world exploration  
- Gamified discovery  
- Fishing‑themed challenges  
- Community-driven rewards  
- Purpose and hope through Fishin 4 Hop3  

Every “catch” is a moment of adventure.  
Every adventure is a step toward something bigger.

---

# 🎯 Mission

To inspire people to:

- Get outdoors  
- Explore their surroundings  
- Build community  
- Find hope through action  
- Support the Fishin 4 Hop3 movement  

Geo Fishin™ is where **adventure meets purpose**.
